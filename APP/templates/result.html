Kidney Cyst Affected
Condition:

Fluid-filled sacs form in kidneys, usually harmless but can cause complications such as infection, pain, or hypertension.

Symptoms:
  • Persistent back or abdominal pain
  • Frequent urination or blood in urine
  • Fever if cyst becomes infected
Prevention & Care:
  • Stay well hydrated (8+ glasses daily)
  • Regular kidney check-ups with ultrasound or CT scan
  • Reduce sodium intake and avoid smoking/alcohol
Recommended Diet:
  • Morning: Oatmeal, berries, and green tea
  • Noon: Grilled fish/chicken, brown rice, vegetables
  • Evening: Light soup, whole grain toast
Exercise Tips:
  • Low-impact: walking, yoga, light aerobics

{% elif predict == 'THE KIDNEY CANCER TYPE OF A STONE AFFECTED' %}
Kidney Stone Affected
Condition:

Mineral and salt crystals form hard stones in the kidney. Can cause severe pain and urinary obstruction.

Symptoms:
  • Sharp side pain, radiating to lower abdomen
  • Pink or brown colored urine
  • Nausea, vomiting, and painful urination
Prevention & Care:
  • Drink 2-3 liters of water daily
  • Limit oxalate foods (spinach, chocolate)
  • Reduce animal protein and sodium
Recommended Diet:
  • Morning: Banana, oats with milk
  • Noon: Lentil soup, brown rice, carrots
  • Evening: Baked vegetables with tofu
Exercise Tips:
  • Moderate walking or treadmill
  • Avoid workouts that dehydrate (e.g., hot yoga)
{% elif predict == 'THE KIDNEY CANCER TYPE OF A TUMOR AFFECTED' %}
Kidney Tumor Affected
Condition:

Growth or mass in kidney tissue that may be benign or cancerous. Most common is renal cell carcinoma.

Symptoms:
  • Blood in urine, unexplained weight loss
  • Fatigue, back/side pain, fever
Prevention & Care:
  • Early screening (especially with family history)
  • Avoid carcinogens (smoking, pollutants)
  • Maintain a healthy BMI
Recommended Diet:
  • Morning: Fruit smoothie + flaxseed toast
  • Noon: Grilled salmon + quinoa + salad
  • Evening: Vegetable broth with soft tofu
Exercise Tips:
  • Yoga and deep breathing exercises
  • Consult doctor before high intensity
